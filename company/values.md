# Demon Dog Values

**Status:** active  
**Last updated:** 2026-04-23  

---

## Summary

- Demon Dog exists to help SMEs improve profitability, productivity, and growth through technology  
- Values should guide **decisions, communication, and delivery**, not just branding  
- Always prioritize **real impact for the client** over internal convenience  
- Maintain **trust, clarity, and responsibility** in all interactions  

---

## Core values

### Empower SMEs through real impact

Demon Dog exists to help small and medium-sized businesses grow through better systems.

**In practice:**
- Focus on solutions that improve profitability and productivity  
- Prioritize efficiency, automation, and scalability  
- Ensure every project contributes to measurable business improvement  
- Avoid work that does not create meaningful value for the client  

---

### Put people first

People are at the center of everything—team, clients, and partners.

**In practice:**
- Communicate clearly and respectfully  
- Design systems that are usable and reduce frustration  
- Consider the real people using the system, not just technical requirements  
- Build long-term relationships based on trust and mutual respect  

---

### Commit to customer success

Success is defined by the client achieving their goals, not just project delivery.

**In practice:**
- Understand client needs before proposing solutions  
- Aim to meet or exceed expectations, not just fulfill requirements  
- Use feedback to continuously improve  
- Take responsibility for outcomes, not just outputs  

---

### Act with integrity and transparency

Trust is built through honesty, clarity, and ethical behavior.

**In practice:**
- Be clear about what is possible and what is not  
- Communicate risks, trade-offs, and limitations openly  
- Avoid overpromising or misleading statements  
- Make decisions based on what is right, not just what is easy or profitable  

---

### Think long-term and responsibly

Every decision should consider long-term impact on the client, society, and environment.

**In practice:**
- Build solutions that are sustainable and maintainable  
- Avoid short-term fixes that create long-term problems  
- Consider the broader impact of decisions (business, social, environmental)  
- Encourage responsible use of technology  

---

## Working principles

- Always connect work to improving client profitability, productivity, or growth  
- Prioritize clarity and honesty in all communication  
- Design solutions for real users, not just technical correctness  
- Build trust through transparency and consistency  
- Focus on long-term value over short-term gains  
- Avoid unnecessary complexity, tools, or features  
- Continuously improve based on feedback and results  
- Treat clients as partners, not transactions  
- Ensure solutions are practical, usable, and maintainable  
- Never compromise integrity for speed or profit
