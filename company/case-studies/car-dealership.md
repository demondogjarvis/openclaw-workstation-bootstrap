# Case Study: Car Dealership ERP & Automation

**Client:** Car Dealership (name withheld)  
**Project:** Odoo Implementation & Custom Modules  
**Status:** draft  
**Last updated:** 2026-04-23  

---

## Summary

- Implemented Odoo as a central system for managing operations, sales, and vehicle data  
- Built custom modules to track true vehicle costs and margins  
- Automated lead handling and first contact using email and WhatsApp  
- Outcome: improved margin visibility, faster lead response, and more structured operations  

---

## About the client

The client is a car dealership managing vehicle sales, inventory, and customer interactions.

Their business depends on:
- Accurate tracking of vehicle costs and margins  
- Efficient handling of incoming leads  
- Fast and consistent customer communication  

---

## The problem

Before the implementation, the dealership faced several challenges:

- No clear overview of true margins per vehicle  
- Costs related to vehicles were not fully tracked or connected  
- Manual handling of incoming leads from email  
- Delays in responding to potential customers  
- Lack of structure in customer communication and follow-up  

This resulted in:
- Reduced control over profitability  
- Missed or delayed sales opportunities  
- Inefficient internal processes  

---

## The solution

Demon Dog implemented Odoo as the central system and extended it with custom functionality tailored to the dealership’s needs.

The solution included:

- Full Odoo setup adapted to the business  
- Custom modules to track all costs related to each vehicle  
- Margin calculation based on real data  
- Automated lead creation from incoming emails  
- Automated WhatsApp messages to welcome and engage new leads  
- Structured handling of customer interactions  

The system was designed to connect sales, operations, and financial insight into one unified workflow.

---

## Key features

- Centralized vehicle and sales management in Odoo  
- Custom cost tracking per vehicle (purchase, repairs, etc.)  
- Real margin visibility per sale  
- Automatic lead creation from email inbox  
- Automated WhatsApp communication for first contact  
- Structured sales pipeline and customer tracking  
- Integration between communication and sales processes  

---

## Outcomes

- Clear visibility into margins for each vehicle  
- Better decision-making based on real profitability data  
- Faster response to incoming leads  
- Increased consistency in customer communication  
- Reduced manual work in lead handling  
- More structured and scalable sales process
