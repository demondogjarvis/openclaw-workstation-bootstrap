# Demondog FAQ

**Status:** active

This document contains reusable, clear answers to common questions about Demon Dog.

---

## What does Demon Dog do?

Demon Dog helps businesses improve how they operate by designing, building, and refining digital systems. This includes custom applications, websites, integrations, and implementations of platforms like CRM, ERP, and cloud infrastructure.

The goal is not just to deliver software, but to solve operational problems—reducing manual work, improving visibility, and creating systems that support growth.

---

## Who is Demon Dog best for?

Demon Dog works best with companies that:
- Have outgrown their current systems or workflows  
- Experience inefficiencies, manual processes, or lack of integration  
- Need reliable, scalable solutions rather than quick fixes  
- Are willing to invest in long-term improvements  

Typical clients are growing small to mid-sized businesses, often with complex operations or ambitions to scale.

---

## How does Demon Dog work?

Demon Dog typically works in a structured but flexible way:

1. **Understand the business**  
   Analyze current processes, challenges, and goals  

2. **Define the solution**  
   Design systems that solve real problems, not just symptoms  

3. **Build and implement**  
   Develop custom components and integrate with existing systems  

4. **Improve over time**  
   Continuously refine and expand the solution as the business evolves  

Where possible, Demon Dog uses reusable, industry-specific building blocks to deliver faster and improve solutions across multiple clients.

---

## What makes Demon Dog different?

- Focus on **business outcomes**, not just technical delivery  
- Combines **custom development with reusable industry-specific solutions**  
- Prioritizes **long-term maintainability over short-term fixes**  
- Works as a **partner**, not just a supplier  
- Strong focus on **efficiency, automation, and scalability**  

Demon Dog aims to deeply understand how a business operates before building anything.

---

## What services does Demon Dog offer?

Core services include:

- Custom software development (web, mobile, backend)  
- API development and system integrations  
- CRM and ERP implementation (e.g. Microsoft Dynamics)  
- Business process automation (e.g. Power Automate)  
- Website development with focus on SEO and performance  
- Cloud infrastructure and architecture (Azure)  
- Technical consulting and system design  

Services are often combined into complete solutions rather than delivered in isolation.
