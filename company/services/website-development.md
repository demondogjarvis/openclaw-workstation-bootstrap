# Website Development

**Status:** active  
**Last updated:** 2026-04-23  

---

## Summary

- Website Development focuses on creating or improving websites that support business goals such as visibility, credibility, lead generation, and sales  
- It is for businesses that need more than just an online presence and want a website that performs as part of their operations and growth  
- The outcome is a professional, effective website built on the platform and level of customization that best fits the client’s needs  

---

## Description

Website Development involves designing, building, improving, or restructuring websites so they support the client’s business in a practical way.

Demon Dog works with a wide range of solutions depending on what fits best, including WordPress, Shopify, custom-built websites, and other suitable platforms. The choice depends on the client’s goals, budget, workflows, growth plans, and need for flexibility.

The goal is not just to create a visually appealing website, but to build a website that performs—whether that means generating leads, supporting sales, strengthening credibility, improving SEO, or integrating with other systems.

---

## Ideal buyer

- Businesses that need a stronger digital presence  
- Companies whose current website no longer supports their goals  
- Organizations that need their website to generate leads, sales, or operational value  
- Businesses looking to connect their website with CRM, ERP, payment systems, or internal workflows  
- Companies that want the right platform for their situation rather than forcing a specific technology  

---

## Problems it solves

- Outdated websites that do not reflect the business properly  
- Poor performance, usability, or mobile experience  
- Websites that do not support lead generation or sales  
- Limited flexibility in the current platform  
- Weak SEO structure or poor discoverability  
- Lack of integration with business systems  
- Difficulty updating or scaling the site over time  

---

## Deliverables

- New website development or major website improvement  
- Platform selection based on business fit (WordPress, Shopify, custom development, etc.)  
- Structure and page planning  
- Frontend and backend implementation where needed  
- E-commerce setup where relevant  
- SEO-focused technical structure  
- Integration with CRM, ERP, payment systems, and other tools where needed  
- Documentation and maintainable setup  

---

## Exclusions

- Ongoing content creation or daily website management unless separately agreed  
- Marketing campaign execution  
- SEO strategy beyond the website’s technical and structural foundation unless separately defined  
- Graphic design-only work without website implementation  
- Forcing a specific platform when another solution is a better fit  

---

## Outcomes

- A website aligned with business goals  
- Improved credibility and professional presence  
- Better lead generation, sales support, or customer engagement  
- Stronger technical foundation for SEO and performance  
- A scalable website solution that can evolve with the business  

---

## Notes for agents

- Position this as **building websites that support business goals**, not just “making websites”  
- Emphasize flexibility in platform choice: WordPress, Shopify, custom development, or other suitable options  
- Avoid implying that Demon Dog always prefers custom development when a standard platform is the better fit  
- Always connect the service to outcomes like visibility, leads, sales, credibility, and operational support  
- Highlight that platform choice should be driven by what best serves the client  
- Where relevant, mention that websites can also be part of a broader system landscape through integrations
